Founder's Note

Welcome to JumpstartCXO where we specialize in helping organizations improve their performance and reach their full potential. My name is Raghav, and I am the Founder and Lead Consultant at our firm.

I have always been passionate about helping organizations to improve their performance and reach their goals. As a Chartered Accountant with a degree in Operations Management, I have always been fascinated by the financial impact of small operational improvements. After years of experience in the consulting industry and working with a diverse range of organizations, I decided to start my own consulting firm to continue my mission.

Our boutique consulting firm is built on the belief that every organization has the potential to perform better and achieve more. We work with our clients to understand their unique challenges and develop tailored solutions that help them to improve their performance and reach their goals - within a reasonable budget.

Our team of experienced consultants is dedicated to delivering results for our clients. We bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise in areas such as process and profitability improvement, performance management, and change management. We are committed to working closely with our clients to understand their needs and deliver solutions that drive real results.

At JumpstartCXO we believe that lasting change comes from within the organization. We work with our clients to build a culture of continuous improvement, where everyone is empowered to contribute to the organization's success.

If you are looking to improve your organization's performance and reach your goals, we would be honored to work with you. Let's build a better future together.

Thank you for visiting our website and I look forward to connecting with you soon.


Sincerely,
Raghav Rathi
Founder and Lead Consultant, JumpstartCXO Consulting
