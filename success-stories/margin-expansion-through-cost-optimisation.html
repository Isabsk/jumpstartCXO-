Margin Expansion through cost optimisation

Profitability Improvement Food and Beverage


The client, a small catering business, was struggling to maintain profitability due to rising costs and stagnant sales. They reached out to our consulting firm for help in improving their profitability.

We began by conducting a thorough assessment of their operations, including financial analysis and a review of their pricing strategy. We identified several areas for improvement, including inefficiencies in their food and labor costs, and a lack of a clear pricing strategy.

Our team worked closely with the owners and employees of the client to develop a comprehensive plan for improvement. We implemented a new inventory management system to better control food costs, and helped them negotiate better deals with suppliers. We also helped them develop a more robust pricing tool that took into account market trends and competition.

In addition, we provided training to the employees on cost-saving techniques and efficient ways of working.

The results were impressive. The client was able to reduce food and labor costs by 20% and increase sales by 15% within the first 6 months of implementing the changes. They were able to reinvest these savings into their business, allowing them to expand their menu and improve their customer service.
